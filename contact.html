About

Neta Rogovsky - Art Curator

I'm Neta Rogovsky, an art curator and lifelong lover of all things creative. Whether I'm curating an exhibition or getting my hands messy with paint, watercolor, or digital design, I love exploring creative mediums. From photography to typography, always trying to find new ways to express myself. For me, art and creativity are ways to express emotions that we cannot put into words.

I created WideWrld to bring together artists and art lovers from around the world. This platform allows creators to share their work, find inspiration, and connect with a supportive community.

WideWrld is the culmination of my belief that art should be accessible, collaborative, and community-driven. Through this platform, I hope to break down barriers in the art world and create a space where creativity can flourish without limitations.
